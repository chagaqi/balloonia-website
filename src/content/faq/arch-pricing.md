Balloon arch pricing in London Ontario depends on size, style, and complexity. A small organic garland arch for a birthday photo spot or classroom starts around $160. A standard doorway or ceremony arch for a wedding or bridal shower typically runs $499 to $749. Large statement arches for venues, grand openings, or full wedding ceremony backdrops can reach $1,200 to $2,400 depending on the design. Geometric and structural arches that require framing sit at the higher end. The best way to get a number is to send us your event type, the space dimensions, your color palette, and any inspiration photos, and Brenda will put together a quote within 24 hours. There is no cost to quote and no pressure to book. Custom designs are our normal, not an upgrade.
