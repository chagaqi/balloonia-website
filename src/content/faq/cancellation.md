---
question: What is your cancellation policy?
pages: ['services', 'contact']
order: 10
---

Cancellations made more than 7 days before the event date receive a 50% refund. Inside 7 days the booking is non-refundable, because by that point materials are sourced, production has started, and the delivery slot is reserved. To reschedule a balloon install, we ask for at least 72 hours notice before the scheduled date and we will check availability to arrange a new date. Weather-related rescheduling is also handled with 72 hours notice. Deposits secure your slot once paid. We ask for a 50% deposit at booking to confirm; events booked less than 7 days out require full payment upfront. Changes and cancellations should be sent in writing via email or DM so there is a clear record. If your event changes scope rather than cancels, message us before the 7-day window and we will adjust the quote where possible.
