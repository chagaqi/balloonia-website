Graduation season is one of our busiest stretches, typically running from May through June in London Ontario. We do balloon arches for backyard grad parties, number columns (Class of 2026 style), large balloon displays for school events and ceremonies, and full outdoor setups for family celebrations. Graduation balloon setups in London usually book up fast in April and early May, so the earlier you reach out, the more options you have for dates and custom designs. We can do school colors, graduation cap themes, photo backdrop walls, and name or number displays for milestone years. If you are planning a grad party for a family member, send us your date, the grad's school colors, and a rough idea of the space, and we will put together a quote. Most grad setups start around $160 for a simple arch and scale up from there for larger installs.
