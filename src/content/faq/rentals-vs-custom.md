Everything we make is custom-built for your event. Balloon installations are not rental items, they are assembled fresh for each booking using new materials matched to your colors, theme, and space. There is no standard inventory you are pulling from. Brenda designs and builds every arch, wall, column, and centerpiece specifically for the event it is going to. This means your balloon decor in London will not look like what showed up at someone else's event last weekend. It is built for your date, your venue, and your color palette. Because each build is custom, pricing is based on size, complexity, and material cost rather than a flat rental fee. For ongoing corporate accounts that want recurring setups, we can build a standing arrangement to keep costs predictable. Message us with your event details and we will quote it accurately the first time.
