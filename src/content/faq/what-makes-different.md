Every install is built from scratch for your event. No catalog arches, no recycled props from a previous booking. Brenda starts with your colors, your venue dimensions, and what you actually want the space to feel like, then builds to that. She also delivers and installs on-site, so your event day has one fewer thing to coordinate. Most balloon orders in London still arrive as DIY kits or off-the-shelf packages. Balloonia is the custom-built alternative.
