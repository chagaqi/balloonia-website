The main difference is Brenda's training. Most balloon vendors in London Ontario are self-taught hobbyists who grew a side hustle. Brenda went through formal professional balloon artistry training and continues working with an active mentor based in Toronto, one of the top balloon artists in the country. That mentorship is live, not a completed course she put on a shelf. It means she has access to technique escalation for complex builds, structural approaches that most local vendors have not seen, and a standard of craft that comes from real professional investment in the work. Beyond training, every installation is custom. No pre-made arches pulled from stock. Every balloon arrangement in London that we deliver is built from scratch to your colors, theme, and space. We also deliver and set up on-site, so your event day has one fewer thing to manage. If you want the most technically trained balloon artist in London, that is Brenda.
