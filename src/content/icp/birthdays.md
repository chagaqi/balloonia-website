We do birthdays at every scale. A balloon stand for the entry table starts at $60. A full milestone party with arch, backdrop, and number column runs $400 to $800. Themed kid parties (carnival, unicorn, dinosaur) and milestone adult parties (30th, 40th, 50th) are our most-booked categories.

For kid parties, send the theme and the kid's age. For milestone parties, send the venue and color palette. Brenda designs the layout, builds it on-site, and is gone before guests arrive.

## What you can book

- Number and letter columns for milestone years
- Themed backdrops (unicorn, dinosaur, carnival, sports)
- Adult milestone arches in jewel tones or metallics
- Photo booth setups
- Themed centerpieces and bouquets
