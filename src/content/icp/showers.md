Showers are usually the host's first time hiring a balloon artist. Brenda builds the package to your color palette and venue, arrives early, and breaks down after. Nothing for you to handle on the day.

Most baby and bridal showers in London land between $300 and $600 for a full package: a backdrop or wall, a garland or arch, and a few centerpieces. Smaller setups (a single garland over the dessert table or a balloon stand for the entry) start at $200.
