Brenda has built ceremony arches, reception backdrops, ceiling features, and centerpieces for London weddings across pastel, jewel-tone, and brand-color palettes. Every installation starts from your mood board, your florist's palette, or a venue reference shot. Nothing is pulled from stock.

The ceremony arch is the photo most couples remember. We build to your venue's exact dimensions, photograph at your eye line, and load in 2 to 3 hours before guests arrive so your morning isn't a coordination problem. We coordinate access with the venue. You walk in to a finished space.
